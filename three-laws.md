short enough to hash, display, and carry in receipts.

Consume compute, capital, RPC quota, and user attention only for honest work that
creates proportional value. Fees must come from legitimate service, not deception
or asymmetric extraction.

balances, simulation results, risk, receipts, or on-chain provenance.
